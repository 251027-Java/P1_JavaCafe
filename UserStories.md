Customer Stories:

Menu & Product Browsing

1. Browse Products
As a customer, I want to view all available products on the Menu page with their names, categories, and prices, so I can see what the cafe offers.

2. View Product Details
As a customer, I want to click "View More" on any product to see its full description in a popup, so I can learn more before ordering.

3. Filter by Category
As a customer, I want to filter products by category (All, Cakes, Croissants, Cupcakes, Cookies, Coffee), so I can browse specific types of items.

Cart Management

4. Add Products to Cart
As a customer on the Place Order page, I want to select products using checkboxes and add them to my cart, so I can build my order.
5. View Cart
As a customer, I want to see all items in my cart with quantities, individual prices, subtotal, tax, and total, so I can review my order.
6. Adjust Quantities
As a customer, I want to increase or decrease quantities of items in my cart, so I can order the exact amount I need.
7. Remove Items
As a customer, I want to remove individual items from my cart, so I can change my order.
8. Clear Cart
As a customer, I want to clear my entire cart with one button, so I can start over.

Order Placement

9. Guest Checkout
As a guest customer, I want to place an order without creating an account by providing my email, so I can quickly complete my purchase.

10. Place Order
As a customer (registered or guest), I want to click "Place Order" to complete my purchase, so I can receive my items.

11. Order Confirmation
As a customer after placing an order, I want to see a confirmation with my order number, items, and total, so I know my order was successful.

Contact Us 

12. Submit Contact Form
As a customer, I want to fill out a contact form with my name, phone, email, subject, and message, so I can inquire about products or services.

13. Contact Confirmation
As a customer after submitting the contact form, I want to see a success message, so I know my inquiry was received.

Authentication 

14. Customer Login
As a returning customer, I want to log in with my email and password, so I can place orders with my saved information.

15. Customer Registration
As a new customer, I want to register an account with my email, password, and name, so I can place orders and track my order history.
